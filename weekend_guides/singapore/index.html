the garden city

This is Singapore. A small, green city state full of cultural experiences and modern architecture. As a melting pot of cultures, Singapore has a multitude of exquisite cuisines waiting to blow your taste buds.

Must do things

  • A. Orchard Road: Singapore's premier shopping and entertainment hub with hundreds of shops and restaurants lined along this 2.2 kilometer long road.
    Price: free
    Hours: 24 hours
    Contact: Orchard Road
    Location: Orchard Road, Singapore
  • B. Marina Bay: An area located near the Downtown core surrounded by countless museums, gardens and beautiful hotels. This area is also filled with bars and clubs like no other!
    Price: free
    Hours: 24 hours
    Contact: Marina Bay
    Location: Marina Bay, Singapore
  • C. Gardens by the Bay: Located in the Marina Bay, Gardens by the Bay contains two huge dome shaped conservatories filled with species of flowers from all over the world as well as man-made Super Trees.
    Price: free to enter, $28 for conservatories
    Hours: 5am-2am everyday (9am-9pm for the conservatories)
    Contact: Gardens by the Bay, +65 6420 6848
    Location: 18 Marina Gardens Drive, Singapore
  • D. Sentosa: An island of fun located right off the coast. Activities include: Universal Studios Singapore, the S.E.A. Aquarium, a water park, indoor skydiving, beaches and much more.
    Price: free to enter, $$ for activities
    Hours: 24 hours, attraction times differ
    Contact: Sentosa, +65 6736 8672
    Location: Sentosa Gateway, Sentosa Island, Singapore
  • E. Night Safari: A nocturnal zoo like no other. Here, you'll be able to see the animals at their most active with the sun out of the way.
    Price: $47
    Hours: 7:30pm-12am everyday
    Contact: Wildlife Reserves Singapore, +65 6269 3411
    Location: 80 Mandai Lake Rd, Singapore

Where to Eat

  • Maxwell Food Center (Tian Tian Chicken Rice / Ah Tai Chicken Rice) ($):Tian Tian and Ah Tai are both extremely famous chicken rice stalls located in Maxwell Food Center. They both often run out of food and have long lines at lunch time. Best to go early!
    Hours: 8am–2am everyday (stalls may have different hours)
    Contact: +65 6225 5632
    Location: 1 Kadayanallur St, Singapore
  • P.S. Cafe ($$): P.S. Cafe never disappoints when it comes to food or desert. The menu varies often and by location and serves a mixture of both western and inspired local dishes.
    Hours: all locations have different hours
    Contact:P.S. Cafe
    Location: multiple locations
  • Sungei Road Laksa ($): Laksa is another famous local dish originating from Chinese and Malay cultures. It’s made from vermicelli, seafood, and a spicy coconut curry. One of the best places to get it is at Sungei Road Laksa.
    Hours: See website for hours
    Contact:Sungei Road Laksa
    Location: Blk 27 Jalan Berseh #01-100, Singapore
  • Cherry Garden ($$$): Located in the Mandarin Oriental Hotel, Cherry Garden is one of the best places to get dim sum in Singapore with wide variety of high quality food.
    Hours: 12-2:30pm & 6:30-10:30pm
    Contact:Mandarin Oriental +65 6885 3500
    Location: 5 Raffles Ave, Singapore
  • Bistro Du Vin ($$): Fantastic authentic French cuisine served at an affordable price in the Orchard road area.
    Hours: 12-2pm & 6:30-10pm
    Contact:Bistro Du Vin +65 6733 7763
    Location: 1 Scotts Rd, #01-14 Shaw Centre, Singapore
